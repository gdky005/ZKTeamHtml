About this template:
ONE is a content focused design, best used for text heavy websites.

Recommended use:
Publishers, news articles and journals.
About side bars / lines on the side of the page:
These side bars are designed to streamline text content structure. You will be able to make the bars longer or shorter as needed in order to properly align your text / paragraph content.

To edit the bars, please press and hold on the bar in HTML Egg, then select "Profile".
